Breonna Taylor

In early 2020, Breonna Taylor and his boyfriend were sleeping when three police officers to execute a search warrant in a drug case. This was a no knock warrant, meaning that the police officers broke in the their house. After breaking in, Breonna's boyfriend fired a gun as he thought an intruder was trying to break in. As a return, policed fired shots where 8 shots got to Breonna, causing her death. Police said that they were looking for drugs, unfortunately, they went to the wrong house (Breonna's house) as the suspect was already found earlier that day. Just like Floyd, a lot of protests happened after this unfortunate death of an aspiring nurse.
